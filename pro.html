Our Solution

SmartPark — End-to-End IoT Intelligence

A complete 6-step flow from sensor to smartphone, automating every touchpoint of the parking experience.

STEP 01
📡
Sense
HC-SR04 ultrasonic sensors on ESP8266 detect vehicle presence in real-time with precision accuracy.
STEP 02
🌐
Connect
NodeMCU transmits data via Wi-Fi to cloud server instantly using MQTT/HTTP protocol.
STEP 03
🗺️
Display
Mobile & web app shows real-time slot availability on a live colour-coded map (green/red).
STEP 04
📅
Book
Users reserve nearest vacant slot with upfront pricing — up to 7 days in advance.
STEP 05
🔲
Enter
QR code scanned at main gate entrance — servo barrier opens automatically for booked vehicles.
STEP 06
💳
Pay & Exit
Payment gateway processes fee; second QR scan opens the exit barrier. LED turns GREEN.

Booking System

QR Gate Flow

From app booking to gate entry and payment exit — fully automated and frictionless.

01
Search & Find
User opens app → sees nearest lots with live vacancy count and price/hr displayed on map.
02
Select & Book
Tap on slot → view amount & duration → Confirm Booking with payment pre-authorization.
03
QR Generated
System generates a unique time-bound QR code for the reserved slot instantly.
04
Gate Entry
QR scanner at entrance reads code → servo barrier opens → slot is reserved & confirmed.
05
Park & Monitor
Sensor confirms car is in correct slot. LED turns RED. App shows live parked status.
06
Exit & Pay
Scan exit QR → payment processed → barrier opens → LED turns GREEN again. Done!

GPIO20 controls servo motor · Opens on valid QR scan · Closes after vehicle passes · Auto-timeout after 10s
Wire Legend
● Yellow — Data TX
● Orange — RX/Trig
● Red — VCC Power
● Green — Echo/Signal
● Black — GND
● Blue — Wi-Fi/MQTT
